Meetings & Workshops

Astrostatistics Summer School (19th)

Penn State Eberly College of Science — June 2024

Successfully completed the 19th Astrostatistics Summer School, an intensive program focused on R programming, data science, data analysis, and astrostatistics. The program included advanced training in statistical methodologies applied to astronomical research, with lectures and hands-on sessions led by experts such as Eric Feigelson and Dr. Jogesh Babu.

LSST AGN Science Collaboration Meeting 2025

Durham University, United Kingdom — July 2025

Participated in the LSST AGN Science Collaboration Meeting held at Durham University. The meeting brought together researchers working on Active Galactic Nuclei within the LSST framework. Scientific discussions covered AGN selection techniques, time-domain science, photometric classification, and preparatory work for LSST Data Release 1.

Engaged in collaborative sessions on galaxy–AGN co-evolution, variability-based selection methods, and data-intensive analysis workflows, gaining insight into large-scale extragalactic survey planning and international collaboration.
